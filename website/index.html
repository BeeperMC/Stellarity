What is FokaStudio's Ender Expansion?



FokaStudio's Ender Expansion (or FSEE for short) is a Minecraft 1.19 worldgen datapack that focuses on rehauling The End dimension in a new, unique and fairly lively way.

FSEE started off as a direct port of Far End, for a server called Azalea Network, but grew into its own independent project as time passed.

Needless to say, it is now nothing like its predecessor. Everything has changed drastically and unless you were to previously play with Far End installed, you wouldn't be able to point out its features in FSEE.
